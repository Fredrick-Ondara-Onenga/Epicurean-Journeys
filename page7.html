Dive in

In lands where sun-scorched earth gives way to dust,
A nation bleeds, a people's hope unjust.
The golden promise, whispered long ago,
Now fractured lies, a bitter, cruelest blow.

The wealthy dine on feasts of gilded grace,
While children weep for crumbs upon the place.
Corruption's grip, a serpent's deadly coil,
Squeezes the life from this once fertile soil.

A wind of change, a promise yet unseen,
Sweeps through the streets, where futures fade between.
The cries of mothers, fathers, young and old,
A story etched in pain, a tale untold.

Yet, even here, where shadows darkly creep,
A spark remains, a promise yet to keep.
For in the hearts of those who fight to rise,
A future blooms beneath these troubled skies.
